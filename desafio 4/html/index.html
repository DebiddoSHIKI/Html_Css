I'm getting tired
Of this technology
We only talk by email
Short and cold messages

Before I get old
I'm going to say everything in poetry.

The at sign is now worthless
It's part of an address
No full stop is even used
Or it even goes at the beginning
Now it's .com If the site is really good
It's worth a high price.
To make matters worse, the language
The emoticon is a risk
There is a symbol for everything
A semicolon and an asterisk
A kiss means
To understand how it looks
Decipher this scribble.

I miss the letters
Written with your own hand
I sent them in June
They only arrived in the summer
But they quenched my longing They were real text
In the lines of the heart.

Now, I write and send
Arriving at the same time
But when I go to chat
The person left
Opened another app
The world was captive
Of the technology of now.

Fortunately, to pray
You don't need the internet
God listens to everyone
If you want, take this test
Two dots are two knees
Your lips are devices
Let God interpret.
